PRIVACY POLICY

Last updated: April 2, 2026

This privacy notice explains how Rabies Reminder handles your information when you use the app.

Rabies Reminder works entirely on your device. It does not connect to the internet and does not send any data outside your device.

SUMMARY OF KEY POINTS

  • We do not collect or store personal information
  • All data remains on your device
  • The app does not use internet connectivity
  • No third-party services or analytics are used
  • You are fully in control of your data

1. INFORMATION YOU PROVIDE

You may enter certain inputs (such as dates for reminders or related details) to use app features.

All such inputs remain stored locally on your device. We do not have access to this information.

2. HOW YOUR DATA IS USED

All app functionality, including reminders and calculations, works entirely on your device.

No data is transmitted to any external servers.

3. NOTIFICATIONS

The app may request permission to send notifications for reminders.

You can disable notifications anytime through your device settings without affecting basic app functionality.

4. DATA SHARING

We do not share any data with third parties.

Your data remains on your device unless you choose to share it manually.

5. DATA STORAGE AND SECURITY

All data is stored locally on your device.

While we design the app with security in mind, no system can be guaranteed to be completely secure. Users are responsible for maintaining the security of their own devices.

6. YOUR CONTROL

  • You can stop using the app at any time
  • You can clear app data via device settings
  • You control what information you enter

7. CHILDREN’S PRIVACY

This app is not intended for children under 13 years of age.

8. UPDATES TO THIS POLICY

We may update this policy from time to time. Updates will be reflected with a revised date.
