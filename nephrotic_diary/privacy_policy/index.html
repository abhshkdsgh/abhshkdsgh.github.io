Privacy Policy

Last updated: May 01, 2026
This Privacy Policy describes how Nephrotic Diary ("we", "us", or "our") handles your information.

1. Fully Offline Operation

Nephrotic Diary is designed to operate entirely offline. The application does not connect to the internet, and we do not have any servers to collect, store, or process your data.

2. Data Storage

All information you enter into the app — including patient names, medical records, and profile details — is stored locally on your device's internal storage.

This data never leaves your device unless you explicitly choose to export it (for example, via CSV export).

3. Permissions

  • Notifications: Used solely for the 7:00 AM daily reminder and download alerts.
  • Storage / Alarms: Used to save exported files and schedule reminders.

4. Data Security

Since your data is stored locally, its security depends on the security of your device. We recommend using device-level encryption and screen locks.

All data is automatically removed from the device if the application is uninstalled.

5. Third-Party Sharing

We do not share, sell, or disclose any information to third parties, as we never collect it in the first place.

6. Your Rights

You have full control over your data. You can review, update, or delete your medical records and profile details directly within the app at any time.
