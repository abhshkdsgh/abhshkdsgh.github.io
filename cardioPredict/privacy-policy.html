PRIVACY POLICY

Last updated: April 2, 2026

This privacy notice explains how CardioPredict handles your information when you use the app.

CardioPredict is designed to work entirely on your device. It does not connect to the internet and does not transmit any data externally.

SUMMARY OF KEY POINTS

  • We do not collect or store personal information
  • All data remains on your device
  • The app does not connect to the internet
  • We do not use third-party analytics or services
  • You are fully in control of your data

1. INFORMATION YOU PROVIDE

You may enter certain inputs (such as lifestyle-related details) to use app features.

All such inputs remain stored locally on your device. We do not have access to this data.

2. HOW YOUR DATA IS USED

All calculations and insights are performed directly on your device.

No data is sent to any server.

3. DATA SHARING

We do not share any data with third parties.

Your data stays on your device unless you choose to share it manually.

4. DATA STORAGE AND SECURITY

All data is stored locally on your device.

While we design the app with security in mind, no system can be guaranteed to be completely secure. Users are responsible for maintaining the security of their own devices.

5. YOUR CONTROL

  • You can stop using the app at any time
  • You can clear app data through device settings
  • You control what information you enter

6. CHILDREN’S PRIVACY

This app is not intended for children under 13 years of age.

7. UPDATES TO THIS POLICY

We may update this policy from time to time. Updates will be reflected with a revised date.
