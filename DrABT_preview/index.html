About Author:

Dr T. Arun Babu is currently working as Professor and Head of the Department of Pediatrics at All India Institute of Medical Sciences (AIIMS), Mangalagiri, Andhra Pradesh, India. He completed MBBS and MD Pediatrics from the prestigious JIPMER, Puducherry. He holds a MBA degree in Hospital Administration and a Diploma in Allergy and Asthma from CMC, Vellore. He has done 'Neonatal Perinatal Medicine' Fellowship at University of Calgary, Canada. He has conducted numerous clinical studies and has published more than 310 research papers in various international and national journals and has more than 1700 citations to his credit. He is best-selling author and has also edited 34 textbooks and contributed chapters in Pediatric textbooks. This includes ‘Pediatrics for Medical Graduates’ book published by ELSEVIER international publishers and ‘TARGET Pediatrics’ MCQ Review book published by Wolters Kluwer Lippincott international publishers. His books have sold more than 2,00,000 copies worldwide till now. He serves as Pediatric Editor at BMC Pediatrics, JIGIMS, 'Frontiers in Pediatrics', Webmedcentral journals and as peer reviewer in numerous Pediatric & Medical journals. He has won the prestigious "Outstanding Medico of the year" Award at 2019 edition of Indian Health Professional Awards, held at Pune, India.
